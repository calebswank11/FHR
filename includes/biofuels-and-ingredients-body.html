Biodiesel

Partnering with Benefuel Inc., a Texas-based company with a patented process for transforming non-food fats and oils into fuels, lubricants and chemicals, Flint Hills Resources operates a biofuels facility in Beatrice, Nebraska. The site is designed to take inedible corn oil, a byproduct of ethanol production, and make biodiesel.
